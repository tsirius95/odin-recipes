Coconut flan

The flan coco or flan antillais is a gourmet dessert that will delight your taste buds. A soft coconut cookie, a creamy vanilla flan and a caramel topping.

Ingredients

  • 125g Coconut
  • 3 eggs
  • 400g sweetened condensed milk
  • 400ml skimmed milk
  • Caramel

Steps

  1. Separate the yolks from the egg whites. Pour the sweetened condensed milk with the same dose of skimmed milk.
  2. Add the coconut and mix everything together.
  3. Beating egg whites. Mix them delicately with the dough.
  4. Caramelize the bottom of the mold.
  5. Bake in the oven in a bain-marie at thermostat 7-8 for 45 minutes.
