Chocolate cake

A chocolate cake is a chocolate-based pastry. There are many recipes around the world. This form of cake is said to have been imagined at the end of the XVIIIᵉ century, after several centuries of chocolate being used solely as a beverage.

Ingredients

  • 200g of pastry chocolate butter
  • 100g butter and a nut for the mold
  • 50g flour
  • 100g powered sugar
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (thermostat 6). In a saucepan , melt the chocolate and the butter cut into pieces over very low heat.
  2. In a salad bowl , add the sugar, eggs, flour. Mix.
  3. Add the chocolate/butter mixture. Mix well.
  4. Butter using a sheet of paper towel and flour your mold then pour the cake batter into it.
  5. Bake for about 20 minutes.
  6. When it comes out of the oven, the cake doesn't seem cooked enough. This is normal, let it cool then unmold it.
